About the Community Portal

Community Portal is a great place to share information and collaborate with people. Moreover, that is key to business processes .i.e. customers, partners, or employees. However, one calls it a portal, a help forum, a support site, HR central, or something else. An online community is a great place to connect with the essential folks in an organization in a new and different way.

PRODUCT

The top most specialized part of the community portal are the products. These are created and modified based upon the administration needs.

CATEGORY

A Category is a subpart of a Product. These categories are classified under different products. A Product may contain one or more Category.

SECTION

A Section is a subpart of a Category. These sections are classified under different categories. A Category may contain one or more Sections.

Article

The Community portal provides help sector information in form of an Article. An article is the most generalized part of the community Portal. A Section is classified into one or more articles.

ADMIN

The Community portal provides admin dashboard where Admins can manage users,roles,products, categories, sections and articles.
